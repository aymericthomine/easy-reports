About Us

Founded by experienced professional from the Investment industry, Molitor Partners is a leading and innovative business introduction catalyst.

Through an exclusive network of independent Council Members, Molitor Partners gives Investment managers access to European qualified investors.

Molitor Partners adresses most specifically Private Equity, Real Estate, Private Debt, Infrastructure and  Hedge Funds Investment Managers.

Value

Molitor Partners provides investment managers with a personalized access to qualified investors across Europe, thanks to the extended network of its introducers community.

The variety and complementarity network of our introducers allows us to arrange highly qualified and targeted meetings with a broad number of European professional investors.

Molitor Partners enables Investment managers to scale up their business development, through a timely and cost efficient solution via a light operational set-up.

Process

Facing a fierce competition in an overcrowded and oversized investment market, Investment managers have difficulties to reach key decision makers. They want to develop new business relationships with qualified investors and open up opportunities at a limited cost and in a limited timeframe.

Molitor Partners enables Investment managers to access reliable first class qualified investors. Molitor Partners aggregates, leads, administrates and rewards a network of independent Council members able to efficiently connect with business contributor anytime in Europe.

Council members run their own independent business and have a privileged access to one or several highly qualified investors. Molitor Partners is convinced that their network are valuable to the community.
